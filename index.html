A valid isbn13 contains the following parts separated by '-' or space (' ') :
ISBN-13 prefix (978 or 979)
1-5 digit group identifier (each digit in range [0-9])
Publisher and title identifiers (can be subdivided into 2 parts, separated by '-' or ' ', having digits in range [0-9])
Check digit (a single digit in range [0-9])

*Please enter isbn13, as isbn10 has been discontinued for years now.
To convert isbn10 to isbn13, prepend '978-' to it.
